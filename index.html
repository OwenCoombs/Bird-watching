What is Birdwatching?

Birdwatching, also known as birding, is a recreational activity that involves observing and identifying birds in their natural habitats. Birdwatchers often use binoculars, field guides, and sometimes cameras to spot and document various bird species. It's a popular pastime enjoyed by people of all ages and backgrounds, and it can be done in various environments, including forests, wetlands, parks, and even urban areas. Birdwatching enthusiasts often appreciate the beauty of birds, their behaviors, and the opportunity to connect with nature.
